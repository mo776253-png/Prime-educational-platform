Course Overview

Biology Course EST 2 is an advanced course that builds upon the fundamentals covered in Biology EST 1. This course delves deeper into molecular biology, biotechnology, and advanced research methods.

Duration

18 weeks (54 hours total)

Level

Advanced

Prerequisites

Biology EST 1 or equivalent

Language

English
