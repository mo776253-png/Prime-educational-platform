Course Overview

Biology Course EST 1 provides a comprehensive introduction to the life sciences, covering fundamental biological concepts, processes, and principles essential for American Diploma students.

Duration

16 weeks (48 hours total)

Level

Intermediate

Certificate

Upon successful completion

Language

English

Module 1: Cell Biology

Learning Objectives
  • Understand cell structure and function
  • Master cellular processes and metabolism
  • Compare prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells
  • Analyze cellular transport mechanisms
Topics Covered
  • Cell Theory and History
  • Prokaryotic vs Eukaryotic Cells
  • Cell Membrane Structure
  • Organelles and Their Functions
  • Cellular Respiration
  • Photosynthesis
  • Cell Division (Mitosis & Meiosis)
  • Protein Synthesis
Key Concepts
Cell Theory: All living organisms are composed of cells, cells are the basic unit of life, and all cells come from pre-existing cells.
