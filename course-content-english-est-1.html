Course Overview

English Course EST 1 provides comprehensive English language instruction designed for American Diploma students. This course focuses on developing all four language skills: reading, writing, speaking, and listening.

Duration

14 weeks (42 hours total)

Level

Intermediate

Certificate

Upon successful completion

Language

English

  • Critical Analysis
  • Summarization Techniques

Assessments & Grading

Assessment Components
  • Writing Assignments: 30%
  • Reading Comprehension Tests: 25%
  • Speaking Presentations: 20%

  • Participation and Attendance: 10%
Grading Scale
  • A: 90-100%
  • B: 80-89%
  • C: 70-79%
  • D: 60-69%
  • F: Below 60%
