Course Overview

Math Course EST 2 is an advanced mathematics course designed for students who have completed Math EST 1. This course delves deeper into calculus, linear algebra, and advanced mathematical concepts.

Duration

16 weeks (48 hours total)

Level

Advanced

Prerequisites

Math EST 1 or equivalent

Language

English

Module 4: Calculus II

Learning Objectives
  • Master integral calculus
  • Understand integration techniques
  • Apply integrals to real problems
  • Solve differential equations
Topics Covered
  • Definite and Indefinite Integrals
  • Integration by Parts
  • Trigonometric Substitution
  • Partial Fractions Integration
  • Applications of Integration
  • Improper Integrals
  • Sequences and Series
  • Power Series and Taylor Series

Module 5: Linear Algebra

Learning Objectives
  • Master vector spaces and subspaces
  • Understand linear transformations
  • Solve eigenvalue problems
  • Apply linear algebra to real problems
Topics Covered
  • Vector Spaces and Subspaces
  • Linear Independence and Basis
  • Linear Transformations
  • Matrix Representations
  • Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors
  • Diagonalization
  • Orthogonal Matrices
  • Applications in Computer Graphics

Module 6: Differential Equations

Learning Objectives
  • Solve first-order differential equations
  • Master second-order linear equations
  • Apply differential equations to modeling
  • Understand Laplace transforms
Topics Covered
  • First-Order Differential Equations
  • Separable Equations
  • Linear First-Order Equations
  • Exact Equations
  • Second-Order Linear Equations
  • Method of Undetermined Coefficients
  • Variation of Parameters
  • Laplace Transforms
