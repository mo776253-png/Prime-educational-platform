Course Overview

Chemistry Course EST 2 is an advanced chemistry course that builds upon fundamental chemical principles. This course covers organic, physical, inorganic, and analytical chemistry with practical applications.

Duration

16 weeks (48 hours total)

Level

Advanced

Prerequisites

Basic Chemistry or equivalent

Language

English
